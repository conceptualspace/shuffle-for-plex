Hi there! I've merged Shuffle with my other Plex extensions, Metatrailer, and Ultrawide Zoom. This extension is no longer maintained, so please visit the Chrome/Firefox store to upgrade now! It is still 100% free and open source.

After installing Enhance-O-Tron, the old versions of Shuffle, Metatrailer, or Ultrawide Zoom will uninstall themselves automatically.
